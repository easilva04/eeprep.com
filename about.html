Our Mission

EE Prep is dedicated to providing high-quality, accessible educational resources for electrical engineering students, professionals, and enthusiasts. We aim to create a comprehensive platform that consolidates electrical engineering knowledge in an easy-to-understand format.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Topics

Our platform covers a wide range of electrical engineering topics, from basic circuit analysis to advanced control systems and digital design. Each topic is explained with clarity and precision, making complex concepts accessible to learners at all levels.

Practice Problems

Reinforce your understanding with our curated collection of practice problems, ranging from fundamental concepts to challenging applications that test your problem-solving skills.

Community-Driven Content

EE Prep is built by engineers for engineers. Our content is continuously reviewed and expanded by a community of electrical engineering professionals, educators, and students.

Our Story

EE Prep began as a collection of study notes and resources compiled by electrical engineering students preparing for exams and professional certifications. Recognizing the need for a centralized, well-organized repository of electrical engineering knowledge, we developed this platform to share these resources with the wider engineering community.

Today, EE Prep continues to grow with contributions from educators, students, and industry professionals who share our passion for electrical engineering education.

Get Involved

EE Prep is an open-source project that thrives on community contributions. Whether you're an experienced engineer, an educator, or a student with fresh perspectives, there are many ways to contribute:

  • Add or improve content on existing topics
  • Create new practice problems
  • Suggest new topics to cover
  • Report errors or suggest improvements

To learn more about how you can contribute, visit our Join & Contribute page.
