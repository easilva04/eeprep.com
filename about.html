About This Site

Learn more about EE Prep and why it was created

Purpose

EE Prep was created to provide free resources for electrical engineering students and anyone interested in learning the subject. The goal is to make learning accessible through simple explanations and practical examples.

How to Use This Site

You can browse topics by subject area or try the practice problems to test your understanding. Most materials include examples and explanations that should be helpful for self-study or as a supplement to course materials.
